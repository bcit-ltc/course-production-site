The Production Board

The Production Board shows all the projects currently in production. Projects are split up into tasks/modules and each task moves through a series of queues within the course production process â€“ e.g. the Estimating Queue, Building Queue, and Quality Assurance (QA) Queue. Generally the tasks are developed in the order received although time sensitive projects can be prioritized.

The Production Board is located on the second floor of the Learning and Teaching Centre (LTC). It is a visual representation of all the tasks that are in our estimation, build, quality assurance (QA), and urgent queues as well as any courses that are on our radar for future production.
